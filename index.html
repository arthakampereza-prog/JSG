01 — Our story

A contractor built on repeat business.

J.S.G Construction Company Ltd started in 2007 with a small team, a pickup and a conviction that Ugandan clients deserved a contractor who finishes what he starts. The first jobs were residential. The referrals that followed turned into commercial buildings, institutional facilities and civil works.

Today we run projects from concept and design through to construction and completion — a complete turnkey service — while keeping the same standards of quality, safety, durability and client satisfaction that got us the second job.

We still believe every successful project begins the same way: a strong partnership, technical excellence, transparency, and attention to the details nobody else notices until it rains.
